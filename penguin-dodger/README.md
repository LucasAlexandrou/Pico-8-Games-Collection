## Penguin Dodger
Dodge orbs shot by evil seals to survive and beat your highscore. 

## Gameplay
![Gameplay](screenshots/penguin_2.gif)

## Controls
- Typical Pico-8 Controls
- Arrow keys to move
- Z/X to traverse menus

## Features
- Highscore system
- Decreasing enemy difficulty
- Music + sounds
- Menu + settings + death screen
